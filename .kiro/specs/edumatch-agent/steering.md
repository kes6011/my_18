# Steering & Vision

## Project Motto
"Closing the Information Gap, One Match at a Time."

## Target Audience
- University students overwhelmed by fragmented notice boards.
- Career-oriented students looking for specific tech-stack training.
- Student project teams seeking financial or mentoring support.

## AI Logic Strategy (Conceptual)
The "Agent" acts as a bridge between the 'Static Notice' and the 'Dynamic User Life'.
- **Input**: Program Schedule, Required Skills, User History.
- **Processing**: Semantic matching of tags + Temporal overlap check.
- **Output**: Actionable items (Join, Resolve Conflict, Apply).
